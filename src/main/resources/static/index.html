Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

From tenant onboarding to rent collection and maintenance tracking — every tool a modern landlord needs.

🏢

Property Management

Add multiple properties with units, track occupancy rates, manage vacant units and get a clear overview of your entire portfolio.

💳

M-Pesa Payments

Tenants pay rent directly via M-Pesa STK Push. Payments are confirmed instantly, receipts generated automatically.

⚠️

Arrears Tracking

Automatically calculates arrears when a tenant underpays. Previous month balances are carried forward and added to the next due amount.

🔧

Maintenance Requests

Tenants report issues directly in the app. Admin and caretaker are notified instantly and can update progress with notes.

🔑

Caretaker Management

Assign caretakers to specific properties. They see only their assigned property — tenants, units and maintenance requests.

📈

Reports & Analytics

Monthly revenue charts, occupancy rates, repair expense tracking and net collections — all in one clear financial overview.

How it works

Three roles,
one platform

Admin, caretaker and tenant each have their own focused dashboard — built for their specific responsibilities.

1

Admin sets up properties

Add buildings, create units, set rent amounts and assign caretakers to properties.

2

Register tenants

Admin or caretaker registers tenants, assigns them to units and sets lease dates.

3

Tenants pay via M-Pesa

Tenant opens the app, sees their balance due and triggers an STK push to their phone.

4

Everyone gets notified

Payment confirmations, maintenance updates and admin notices are delivered in real time.

📅

Payment window enforcement

Admin sets allowed payment dates (e.g. 1st–5th). Late payments are flagged and require a reason.

🔄

Automatic arrears carry-forward

When a tenant underpays, the balance is tracked and automatically added to the next month's due amount.

💳

Credit balance management

Overpayments are saved as credit and automatically deducted from the following month's rent.

📱

Instant STK Push

Tenant pays directly from their phone via M-Pesa prompt. No manual transfers, no confusion.
